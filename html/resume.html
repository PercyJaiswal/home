Major Projects

Real Estate key box

Complete mechanical and electrical redesign of a Real Estate keybox to add BTLE capability, stronger motor drive mechanism, extended battery life capability of 8 years and a stronger mechanical body to make it more rugged. This device had a plastic engraved Bluetooth antenna.

New product design - A miniature sized commercial hotel lock

This was a new product design for the next-gen hotel lock series in client's product line. At its launch, it will be one of the smallest hotel locks available in the industry. Some of the challenges in this design the was use of a patented Cap Sense technology to wake up lock from sleep, BTLE, RFID and Cap Sense co-existence and futuristic looking LED lighting with light pipes, all done with minimal battery usage.

Redesign of a Cabinet Lock Control board

This project was to upgrade an existing Cabinet lock control board PCB. It was getting upgraded with BTLE capability as well as a charge pump circuitry to handle bigger locks.

Development of IoT Sensor nodes for Service Improvement

This project involved design and development of IoT sensor nodes which were to be used in the Elevator maintenance / repair system. Selection and design of different sensor nodes like Motion Detector (PIR), Vibration, Luminance, Microphone, Oil level detector and Time of Flight MEMS sensor. Each sensor node had its own local power supply and signal conditioning circuit on board. Performing pre-compliance EMI / EMC testing on all boards.

Design of 100W power supply for an HVAC control board

Th scope of this project involved design and prototyping of Power supply module for a control unit which was to be used in an HVAC system. In the first stage, an initial power budget was calculated for the complete product. Then in the second stage, a multi-stage Power Suppy Unit was designed consisting of Bridge Rectifier, a Buck-Boost and multiple Buck converter circuits with external switches and snubber circuits.

Engineering support for Aircraft's Slat Flap Electronics Circuit Unit (SFECU)

Part of a team to come up with and perform acceptance testing for SFECU from onsite. Activities involved understanding of the design and coming up with test cases for different PCBs, identifying bugs in the existing design, participating in finding solutions using Fault Tree Analysis and validating the proposed solution on engineering prototypes. Come up with plans to automate testing to reduce the time required and errors in the testing process.

73K222AL Modem Emulation Board Design

The scope of this project was to design a custom board that would act as an exact hardware and software drop-in replacement for obsolete modem IC 73K222AL from TDK. This project involved a microprocessor (for communication and configurations), FPGA (to implement different modem protocols), Dual output linear regulator and Analog signal conditioning circuitry.

RoHS Analysis

This was my first onsite experience. Lead Electrical side of client's effort in getting their product line RoHS compliant. The Project involved scrubbing the complete product line for electrical parts. Identifying non-RoHS-compliant parts, searching for its alternates and prototyping them.

Remote pressure monitoring

This project involved devloping a sensor interface board to convert the ratiometric output from remote pressure sensors to multiple analog outputs like 4-20mA loop current, 0-5V, 1-5V and 0-10V outputs. The board had to accept a wide variety of input voltage from 18-230V AC to 15-30V DC. This design involved Half wave rectifier PSU, Differential amplifier and 4-20mA regulating analog circuitry amongst its major sections.
